## French Caribbean — No Exchange Needed! 🇫🇷

Sending to **Martinique, Guadeloupe, Saint Martin, or Saint Barthélemy**?

These are French overseas territories — they use the **Euro (€)**.

You can transfer directly via:
- SEPA transfer (free or very low cost)
- Your regular French bank
- Instant payment apps (Paylib, Lydia)

No currency conversion needed. Just a standard Euro transfer.

## FAQ

**Is Wise available in France?**
Yes, Wise is fully licensed and popular in France. You can open an account online in minutes.

**What's the cheapest way to send to Martinique?**
SEPA transfer from any French bank — it's Euro to Euro, so no exchange needed.

**Can I send cash pickup to Jamaica from France?**
Yes, Western Union and MoneyGram offer cash pickup throughout Jamaica.

**How long does a transfer to Barbados take?**
Typically 1-2 business days with Wise. Bank transfers may take 3-5 days.
