## Dutch Caribbean — Special Cases 🇳🇱

### Bonaire, Saba, Sint Eustatius (BES Islands)
These are **special municipalities of the Netherlands** — they use the **US Dollar (USD)**.

You'll need EUR → USD conversion.

### Aruba
Uses the **Aruban Florin (AWG)** — pegged to USD at 1.79:1.

### Curaçao & Sint Maarten
Use the **Netherlands Antillean Guilder (ANG)** — pegged to USD at 1.79:1.

**Pro tip:** Because AWG and ANG are pegged to USD, EUR/USD moves affect your rate significantly.

## Tips for Sending from Netherlands

1. **Wise usually wins** — transparent and cheap
2. **Bunq Premium users** — check your included transfers
3. **For Bonaire/Saba/Statia** — you're sending USD, time EUR/USD well
4. **Large amounts (€10,000+)** — call your bank for negotiated rates
5. **Regular family support** — set up recurring transfers for better rates
