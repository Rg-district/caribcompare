## 4. Wise/Frankfurter Rate APIs

### Frankfurter API (api.frankfurter.app)
- **Status: ❌ Does NOT support BBD (Barbadian Dollar)**
- The API only covers 30 major ECB/IMF currencies: AUD, BRL, CAD, CHF, CNY, CZK, DKK, EUR, GBP, HKD, HUF, IDR, ILS, INR, ISK, JPY, KRW, MXN, MYR, NOK, NZD, PHP, PLN, RON, SEK, SGD, THB, TRY, USD, ZAR
- BBD is not on this list. The endpoint returns `{"message":"not found"}` for GBP→BBD
- **Use the Frankfurter API for EUR/GBP/USD cross-rates only**

### Current GBP/BBD Rate
- **£1 GBP = approximately $2.6715 BBD** (XE.com mid-market rate, March 2026)
- Note: BBD is **pegged to USD at exactly 2:1** (1 USD = 2 BBD always). So GBP/BBD moves exactly with GBP/USD.
- **Strategic insight:** Since BBD is USD-pegged, you only need GBP/USD to calculate GBP/BBD (multiply GBP/USD × 2). You can use Frankfurter for GBP/USD and multiply by 2 for BBD!

### Better Free APIs for BBD/Caribbean currencies

| API | BBD Support | Rate Limits | Cost | Notes |
|-----|------------|-------------|------|-------|
| **fawazahmed0/exchange-api** (GitHub) | ✅ Yes | None | Free | 200+ currencies, hosted on GitHub CDN. Best free option. URL: `https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@fawazahmed0/currency-api@latest/v1/currencies/gbp.json` |
| **ExchangeRate-API.com** | ✅ Yes | 1,500 req/month free | Free tier | All world currencies. JSON. Easy to use. |
| **Open Exchange Rates** | ✅ Yes | 1,000 req/month free | Free tier | 170+ currencies. Well-documented. |
| **Frankfurter** | ❌ No | Unlimited | Free | Only 30 major currencies — not suitable for Caribbean |
| **Wise API** (rates endpoint) | ✅ Yes | Requires partnership | Free with affiliate status | Live mid-market rates + Wise's actual send rates. Most accurate for comparison. |
| **XE.com API** | ✅ Yes | Paid only | ~$199/month | Professional grade; overkill for MVP |

**Recommendation for CaribCompare:**
1. **MVP:** Use `fawazahmed0/exchange-api` (GitHub CDN) — completely free, 200+ currencies, no rate limits
2. **With Wise affiliate status:** Integrate Wise API for live comparison rates — shows actual transfer amounts, not just mid-market
3. **Formula for BBD:** `GBP/BBD = GBP/USD × 2` (because BBD is USD-pegged 2:1)

## 5. UK→Caribbean Remittance Market

### Barbados Remittance Data
- **Total remittances received (2022–2023):** $85.18 million USD/year (World Bank via TheGlobalEconomy.com)
- **As % of GDP:** 1.33% of GDP in 2023 (down from 1.46% in 2022)
- **Barbados GDP (2024):** $7.17 billion USD (World Bank)
- Context: Barbados is a small island nation of ~280,000 people — $85M in remittances is significant per capita

### UK Bajan Diaspora
- **2001 UK Census:** 21,601 people born in Barbados living in the UK
- UK is the **2nd largest Bajan diaspora** (after the USA, which had 53,785 Barbadian-born residents)
- Migration from Barbados to UK between 1951–1971 = 12% of Barbados's population
- Including 2nd/3rd generation descendants, the total **UK Bajan community is estimated at 100,000+**
- Windrush generation + children + grandchildren = substantial UK-based community with ongoing ties to Barbados

### UK International Remittance Market
- **UK total outbound remittances (2023):** $4.13 billion USD
- **Forecast 2028:** $5.41 billion USD (5.0% CAGR)
- UK is one of the world's top remittance-sending countries
- African diaspora is the fastest-growing segment, but Caribbean diaspora is well-established

### Key Providers for UK→Barbados Corridor
Based on Monito's data (18 providers compared for Barbados):
1. **TransferGo** — cheapest most often (34% of searches, per Monito)
2. **Wise** — among cheapest; fees from 0.41%, competitive rates (per Finder UK)
3. **WorldRemit** — major player, cash pickup option available
4. **Remitly** — solid corridor coverage, competitive rates
5. **Western Union** — expensive but ubiquitous, cash pickup
6. **MoneyGram** — similar to WU, declining market share
7. **XE Money** — strong rates, good for larger amounts

### Average Transfer Amounts
- No specific Barbados data found, but Caribbean remittance averages typically £100–£500 per transfer
- UK→Caribbean average: likely £200–£400/transfer based on regional data
- Higher-value transfers (£1,000+) common for property, business, or education purposes

### Market Share Observations
- Digital providers (Wise, WorldRemit, Remitly) taking share from traditional (WU, MoneyGram)
- Cash pickup still relevant in Barbados (Western Union/MoneyGram)
- No single dominant player for UK→Barbados specifically
